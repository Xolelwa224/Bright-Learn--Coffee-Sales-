☕ Bright Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

Executive Business Intelligence & Data Analytics Case Study

This project presents a comprehensive sales analysis of historical transactional data from 'Bright Coffee Shop'. The objective is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that support executive decision-making and help the newly appointed CEO improve revenue, optimize product performance, and drive business growth.

The analysis follows a complete end-to-end data analytics workflow, including project planning, data processing, SQL analysis, dashboard development, data storytelling, and executive reporting.

🎯 Project Objective

The primary objective of this project is to use data analytics, SQL, and visualization techniques to answer key business questions and provide actionable recommendations.

Business Questions

* Which products generate the highest revenue?
* Which products sell the most units?
* What are the peak and slowest sales periods?
* How do sales trends change across different products and time intervals?
* What strategies can improve overall sales performance and profitability?

📋 Project Tasks

Task 1: Project Planning & Architecture
* Designed a complete data flow and ETL architecture.
* Documented the analytics workflow from raw data to executive dashboard.
* Defined business objectives, KPIs, and analytical requirements.
* Planned data storage, transformation, analysis, and reporting processes.

Task 2: Data Processing & SQL Analysis
* Converted the raw Excel dataset into CSV format.
* Imported the dataset into Databricks.
* Cleaned and transformed transactional data.
* Standardized unit price formatting.
* Created transaction time buckets for time-based analysis.
* Calculated 'Total Revenue' using:
sql
Total Amount = Unit Price × Transaction Quantity

* Grouped transactions by:
  * Product Category
  * Product Type
  * Product Detail
  * Time Buckets
  * Sales Intervals

💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following strategic recommendations are provided:
* Increase inventory levels for high-performing products.
* Launch targeted promotions during slower trading periods.
* Improve visibility of underperforming products through bundled offers.
* Optimize staff scheduling during peak customer hours.
* Introduce customer loyalty programs to encourage repeat purchases.
* Automate sales reporting to support faster executive decision-making.
* Expand performance tracking for future multi-store operations.
